Why categorization is hard

Same vendor. Different category. Every time it depends on context.

Amazon could be Office Supplies, Software, Equipment, or Cost of Goods. It depends on what was bought. The same client might have two Amazon accounts for two entirely different purposes. A rule-based system cannot handle that. A trained AI can.

The reason categorization takes so long is not that it is difficult. It is that every transaction requires a small judgment call, and those judgment calls add up to hundreds of hours across a year of client work.
