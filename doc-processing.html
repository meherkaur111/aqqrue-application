The Problem

The average invoice takes 4 minutes to process manually. Aqqrue takes 4 seconds.

Across a 50-client firm, that is roughly 8 hours every week that goes into reading documents and typing what they say into another system. We are not judging. We are just saying there is a better use for those 8 hours.

A receipt is just a structured piece of information. A bank statement is a structured piece of information. An invoice is a structured piece of information. None of them need a human being to read them first.

How It Works

Drop it in. Walk away. Come back to clean data.

Step 01
Drop the document
Email it, upload it, or let Aqqrue pull it directly from your client portal, Google Drive, or Dropbox. Any format. Any layout.
Step 02
AI reads every line
Optical character recognition combined with document understanding models. It does not just extract text, it understands context. A $500 figure means different things in different positions on a document.
Step 03
Data lands in your books
Every field populates directly into QuickBooks or Xero. Vendor, amount, date, category suggestion, line items. You review exceptions. That is your only job here.

Accuracy

What happens when it is not sure?

We designed Aqqrue around a simple principle: a wrong automatic entry is worse than a flagged manual one. When confidence is below threshold, the document is not silently filed. It is surfaced to you with the extracted data pre-filled and the specific uncertainty highlighted.

The result: you spend time reviewing edge cases, not reading every document. The ratio, for a typical firm, is about 94 documents handled automatically for every 6 that need your eyes.
