FAQ

The questions you are
probably about to ask.

Can I cancel anytime?⌄
Yes. Cancel from your dashboard at any time. No contracts, no cancellation fees, no calls with a retention specialist trying to change your mind. If you cancel, you keep access until the end of your billing period.
Is there a free trial?⌄
Yes. All plans include a 14-day free trial. No credit card required. We want you to see results before you commit, not after.
What accounting software does Aqqrue connect to?⌄
QuickBooks, Xero, and Sage are fully supported. More integrations are in progress, including FreshBooks and Zoho Books. If your stack is not on this list, reach out and we will let you know the timeline.
How long does setup take?⌄
Most firms connect their stack and go live within five minutes. Full automation accuracy kicks in within the first week as Aqqrue learns your firm's categorization patterns. By day thirty, it runs your close faster than your current process does.
