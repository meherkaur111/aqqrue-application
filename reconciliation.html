How it works

Continuous reconciliation. Not a monthly panic.

Continuous
Matches as entries arrive
Aqqrue does not wait until month end. Every time a bank transaction arrives, it tries to match it immediately. By the 30th, most of the work is already done.
Handles complexity
Partial payments, splits, multi-currency
A client pays invoice #4421 in two installments across different accounts. Aqqrue recognizes the pattern, links both payments to the invoice, and marks it settled. No manual tracking.
Exceptions only
You see what matters
Unmatched items are surfaced with full context: the bank line, the best candidate match, the confidence score, and why it did not auto-match. You decide in one click, not after thirty minutes of digging.

What gets flagged for review

The 6% your team actually needs to look at.

When Aqqrue cannot match with sufficient confidence, it tells you exactly why. These are the only things that land in your review queue.

  • ⚠
    Amount mismatch above threshold
    Invoice says $5,000. Bank shows $4,950. Possible early payment discount or partial payment. Flagged for your call.
  • ⚠
    New bank account or entity
    Transfer to an account Aqqrue has not seen before. Could be a new vendor, a new internal account, or an error. Your eyes needed.
  • ⚠
    Timing gap exceeds normal pattern
    Payment received 47 days after invoice date when client normally pays in 30. Flagged in case there is a collections issue worth noting.
  • ⚠
    No matching invoice found
    Bank shows a payment but no corresponding invoice exists in the system. Possible missing document, possible erroneous charge.
